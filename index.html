Services

Our wide range of services ensures that you receive the best possible care in the comfort of your own home, promoting health, independence, and peace of mind. Explore our services designed to support your journey to better health.

Skilled Nursing Care

Our experienced registered nurses provide skilled nursing services, including wound care, medication management, and symptom monitoring, ensuring optimal recovery and management of health conditions.

Physical Therapy:

Our licensed physical therapists develop personalized exercise programs and rehabilitation techniques to improve mobility, strength, and function, empowering you to regain independence and quality of life.

Occupational Therapy:

Our certified occupational therapists focus on enhancing daily living skills and independence through customized interventions, adaptive equipment training, and environmental modifications, helping you perform daily activities with ease and confidence.

Speech Therapy

Our speech-language pathologists assess and treat communication and swallowing disorders, offering tailored therapy plans to improve speech, language, cognition, and swallowing function, promoting effective communication and safe eating habits.

Emergency Transport Services

Our reliable transportation ensures safe and efficient transfers to medical facilities in case of emergencies, providing peace of mind during critical situations.

Supply of Home Health Equipment

We offer a wide range of high-quality medical equipment and supplies, including mobility aids, respiratory equipment, and wound care supplies, delivered directly to your home for convenience and comfort.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What types of home health services do you offer?

    We offer a wide range of services including skilled nursing care, physical therapy, occupational therapy, speech therapy, emergency transport services, and the supply of home health equipment.

  • How do I know which care plan is right for me?

    Our team will conduct a comprehensive assessment of your health needs and preferences to recommend the most suitable care plan. We work closely with you to ensure your chosen plan aligns with your specific requirements.

  • Are your services available 24/7?

    Yes, we provide around-the-clock care, including nights, weekends, and holidays, ensuring you have access to support whenever you need it.

  • Do you offer telehealth consultations?

    Yes, we offer convenient telehealth services, allowing you to receive medical advice and care management from the comfort of your home through virtual consultations.

  • How do I get started with 247HomeHealth.net?

    Simply contact us through our website or call our office. Our team will guide you through the initial assessment process, help you choose the right care plan, and set up your services to start receiving care at home.
